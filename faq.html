FAQ

  • HOW CAN I GET A YOLLANDO ADDRESS IN TURKEY?
    Click the Sign Up Now button the home screen, select the membership option that suits you the most and fill our simple and easy registration form. As simple as that.
  • CAN I USE MY YOLLANDO ADDRESS AS SOON AS A REGISTER?
    Yes, you can use your Yollando address directly after the registration. As simple as that.
  • HOW CAN I DO SHOPPING WITH MY YOLLANDO ADDRESS ?
    You need to enter your Yollando address as the delivery address at check out when do shopping from any online store.Pleases see the example below.
  • HOW CAN I SHIP MY PACKAGES THAT CAME TO YOLLANDO?
    Log in to your Yollando account. Check whether your packages have reached us or not from “my packages” You can send your packages as one pieces each or you can send them all together. After selecting your package click on to the “Next” button, write your shipping adress then go to the payment page. Choose the shipping company you want and make the payment. Note: You can track your package from “Sent packages” under “My packages” menu in your Yollando account.
